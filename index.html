This is our story



The brand has a fresh minimal approach to design, materials and all aspects of the brand. The aim is to minimise steps and to keep as much of the production in Greenwich as possible.

Cutting the middleman allows us to offer a simple pricing structure that is fair on everyone without compromising on quality.

Sourcing the leather directly from tanneries in the UK and Italy also supports our approach.

Ryan established the company when he made his first phone case in 2013 while studying in London. The brand spread quickly amongst friends and colleagues through word of mouth.
